Building Materials question figures
===================================

  fig9.png, fig10.png, fig15.png, fig16.png, fig21.png, fig24.png, fig29.png, fig38.png

Wrong: "fig 9.png"   Right: "fig9.png"

Questions that use diagrams are listed in data/building-materials.json (image field).
